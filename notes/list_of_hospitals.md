# Hospital Price Transparency List

- **Type:** Academic medical center
- **Ownership/System:** Independent, nonprofit academic health system
- **Price transparency:** Vanderbilt "Estimates and Price Transparency" page. Vanderbilt describes itself as the most comprehensive academic health system in the Southeast.

- **Type:** Regional teaching / tertiary hospital
- **Ownership/System:** Independent public academic system
- **Price transparency:** Erlanger price transparency page with machine-readable files for Baroness and other Erlanger hospitals.

- **Ownership/System:** Ballad Health

- **Type:** Community hospital
- **Ownership/System:** Ballad Health

### TriStar Centennial Medical Center — Nashville, TN

### TriStar Hendersonville Medical Center — Hendersonville, TN
