### Variation & Spread

- How much do prices vary for the same procedure across hospitals in a market (city, region, state)?
- What is the range between the cheapest and most expensive hospital for common, shoppable procedures?
- Which "shoppable" or common services are most useful for employers and consumers to compare? (High range)
- Which procedures show the most price variation vs. which are relatively uniform?

### Payer Dynamics

- How do negotiated rates differ across payers (BCBS vs. Aetna vs. UHC) at the same hospital for the same procedure?
- Which payer(s) appear to receive the best or worst negotiated rates?
- Which payers consistently negotiate the lowest rates? The highest?
- How does the cash/self-pay rate compare to negotiated rates? is it ever competitive?

### Hospital Behavior

- Do hospital systems (multi-hospital orgs) show consistent pricing across their facilities, or do individual hospitals vary?
- Are higher-priced hospitals also higher quality (would need quality data)?
- How do large urban academic medical centers compare to community hospitals and critical access hospitals?

### Compliance

- Are hospitals publishing rates in ways that reduce price transparency?
- Which hospitals appear least standardized or least compliant?
